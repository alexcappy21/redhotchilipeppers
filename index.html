I love the Red Hot Chili Peppers!



Bio

The Red Hot Chili Peppers were formed in Los Angeles, California. Three high school friends, Anthony Kiedis, Michael "Flea" Balzary and Hillel Slovak, started the band in 1983. They are now conidered the most successful band in alternative rock history. They have 13 number-one singles and have on 6 Grammy awards. They were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2012.
